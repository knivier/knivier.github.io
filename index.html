About Me

Hi, I'm Agniva (Knivier)!

I'm a high school junior that loves computers. Currently, my targetted major is Computer Engineering as I've always been interested in computers and how they work from the ground up. I also love specializing in embedded systems and OS development. Basically everything low-level is fun. Sometimes, I go crazy with my abstracted projects (like my portfolio).

I specialize in Java/Rust/C Development, unity between hardware and software, and OS development. When I'm not coding, you'll find me playing tennis, Minecraft, or going through a hike in the mountains.

Note: Not all of my projects are available on here nor GitHub due to privacy reasons. I've worked with some universities and the data is not public, meaning the projects cannot be public either. Feel free to contact me if you'd like to learn more about what I do though!
