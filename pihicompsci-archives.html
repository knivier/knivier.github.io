About the Archives

The PiHi Computer Science Club is dedicated to fostering a passion for programming, problem-solving, and technology among high school students. We provide a collaborative environment where students can learn, create, and grow their technical skills through hands-on projects and peer learning.

The archive project aims to document the club's activities, leadership, and achievements over the years. Some information is redacted for the privacy of others. I try to monitor the archives for personal information and redact them accordingly.

Some content, like photos and logos or other similar things, are kept in the archives. These do not leak the personal identity of anyone; however, if you want to have your content removed, please contact me. These archives are kept self-mantained for now by me (Agniva G, 2023-26 term) and while I will try to keep this website up, the most accurate place you can get information is still the official website, which you can find at www.pihicompsci.org assuming it's still up.
